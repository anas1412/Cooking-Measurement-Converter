Common Measurement Equivalents

Volume Measurements:

  • 1 Cup = 240 ml
  • 1 Tablespoon = 15 ml
  • 1 Teaspoon = 5 ml
  • 1 Ladle = 120 ml

Weight Measurements:

  • 1 Kilogram = 1000 grams
  • 1 Pound = 453.6 grams
  • 1 Ounce = 28.35 grams
  • 1 Yogurt Container = 150 grams

Tips

  • For precise baking, use weight measurements (grams) instead of volume
  • The yogurt container measurement is based on a standard 5.3 oz container
  • Liquid ingredients should be measured using volume units (ml, l)
  • Dry ingredients are best measured using weight units (g, kg)
